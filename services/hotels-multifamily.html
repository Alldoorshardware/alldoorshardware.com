Hotels & Multi-Family Solutions

Purpose-Built Door, Hardware & Security Packages for Hospitality and Residential Properties

Hotels, apartment communities, condominiums, and student housing all share a common challenge: hundreds of door openings that must be secure, durable, code-compliant, and guest- or resident-friendly — often on tight budgets and aggressive construction timelines. All Doors Hardware specializes in coordinated packages that bring together doors, frames, hardware, access control, and surveillance into a single, streamlined solution for high-occupancy properties.

Guest Room & Unit Entry Systems

The entry door is the first impression for every guest or resident. We supply complete entry packages including fire-rated doors and frames, electronic lock systems, peepholes, door closers, and thresholds — all specified to work together and meet local fire and building codes. Our hotel lock solutions support RFID key cards, mobile credentials, and integration with property management systems (PMS) for seamless check-in and room assignment.

  • RFID, mobile key, and smart lock systems
  • PMS integration (Opera, Mews, etc.)
  • Fire-rated entry assemblies (up to 90 min)
  • STC-rated doors for acoustic privacy

Common Area & Corridor Hardware

Lobbies, hallways, stairwells, fitness centers, pool areas, and parking garages all require different hardware solutions based on traffic volume, security level, and fire-code requirements. We provide complete hardware sets for every common-area opening — from heavy-duty exit devices and magnetic hold-opens in corridors to weatherproof hardware for exterior and rooftop doors. Every product is specified for the cycle count and abuse level typical of multi-occupant buildings.

  • High-traffic exit devices and closers
  • Magnetic hold-open and release devices
  • ADA-compliant levers and operators
  • Stairwell and parking garage hardware

Building-Wide Access Control & Surveillance

Managing access across an entire property — from the front entrance and elevators to amenity spaces, back-of-house areas, and maintenance rooms — requires a unified system. We design and supply access control platforms that use a single credential across every controlled door, with zone-based permissions, time-restricted access, and full audit logging. Pair this with our CCTV solutions for a complete property security package.

  • Unified credential across all doors
  • Elevator and amenity access integration
  • IP camera systems for lobbies and perimeters
  • Cloud-based management and remote access

Turnkey Packages & Project Support

Outfitting a 200-room hotel or a 300-unit apartment building means coordinating hundreds of doors, thousands of hardware items, and multiple trades — all on a construction schedule that doesn't wait. All Doors Hardware provides turnkey packages with complete door, frame, and hardware schedules, phased delivery to match your construction timeline, and dedicated project management from spec review through final punch list. We simplify the process so you can focus on building.

  • Complete door and hardware schedules
  • Phased delivery to match construction
  • Dedicated project manager
  • Value engineering for budget optimization
