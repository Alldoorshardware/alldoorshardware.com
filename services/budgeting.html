Budgeting & Project Management

Expert Planning Services to Keep Your Project On Track & On Budget

Great projects don't happen by accident — they're planned. All Doors Hardware provides comprehensive budgeting, estimating, and project management services that take the guesswork out of your commercial door and hardware installations. From initial blueprint review through final punch list, our team ensures your project stays on schedule, on budget, and fully compliant with specifications and codes.

Blueprint & Specification Review

Every successful project starts with a thorough review. Our estimating team analyzes your architectural drawings, door schedules, and hardware specifications to identify the products needed, flag potential issues early, and ensure every opening meets fire, life safety, accessibility, and security codes. We catch problems on paper so they don't show up on the job site.

  • Complete door schedule analysis
  • Hardware specification review and verification
  • Fire rating and code compliance checks
  • Identification of missing or conflicting specifications

Accurate Estimating & Budgeting

Our detailed estimates give you full visibility into material costs, lead times, and budget impact before you commit. We provide itemized pricing with alternatives at different price points so you can make informed decisions. Whether you're in the pre-construction budgeting phase or finalizing purchase orders, our estimates are accurate, transparent, and competitive.

  • Itemized pricing with brand and model detail
  • Good-better-best budget alternatives
  • Lead time and availability reporting
  • Value engineering recommendations

Project Coordination & Logistics

Keeping doors and hardware aligned with your construction schedule requires active coordination. Our project management team tracks manufacturing, shipping, and delivery timelines across all vendors and coordinates with your general contractor to ensure materials arrive on site when they're needed — not too early, not too late. We manage the logistics so your crew stays productive.

  • Vendor and manufacturer coordination
  • Phased delivery scheduling
  • Site delivery and staging coordination
  • Real-time order tracking and updates

Punch List & Closeout Support

The project isn't done until every door opens, closes, locks, and latches correctly. Our team supports you through the final punch list process, addressing warranty claims, backordered items, and last-minute adjustments. We also provide project closeout documentation including as-built hardware schedules, warranty information, and maintenance guidelines to support your facility team long after occupancy.

  • Punch list inspection support
  • Warranty claim management
  • As-built documentation and hardware schedules
  • Ongoing post-occupancy support
